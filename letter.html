My dearest,

Two years ago, I didn't know that one quiet afternoon would turn into the start of something I'd never want to end. Since then, every ordinary day with you has somehow turned into a memory I want to keep.

I love the way you get lost in your own little world, the way you laugh at the things only you find funny, and the way you make even sitting on a bench under the trees feel like the most peaceful place in the world. You have this quiet way of making everything feel softer, calmer, better — just by being there.

These two years haven't been perfect, but they've been ours, and that's what makes them mean everything to me. Through the good days and the hard ones, you've stayed — and I don't think I'll ever stop being thankful for that.

So here's to us, to the two years behind us, and to every year still waiting for us. Thank you for loving me, for staying, and for being exactly who you are.

I love you. Today, and always.

— yours, always Jexter
